What you get when you sign in

Your own academic data, read with your own login and kept to your account.

  • Degree Audit — your major & college requirements, earned vs. still needed.
  • Degree Planner — plan quarter-by-quarter, automatically checked against prerequisites.
  • General Catalog — search Fall 2026 courses with your own prereq status shown.
  • GPA Calculator — project your GPA or find the grades you need.
  • Academic History — import every course you've taken and what's in progress.
  • The classic WebReg emulator — still included, exactly the way you remember it.
